About our business

Before the rise of the internet, people used to rely on vinyl records, CDs, and cassette tapes to listen to music, which was cumbersome. In this day and age, where technology is at its peak. Online streaming platforms like Spotify bring convenience in listening to music. However, they stripped away the immersive experience from the physical media. We recognized that many still crave the authentic experience of listening to music. We identified a niche market, and after a lengthy consideration, we have decided to open Track Zero. The products that Track Zero offers include vinyls, CDs, and cassette tapes, all on one platform. Through its online store, Track Zero provides customers with online services including detailed product information and personalized recommendations. The scope of Track Zero includes online sales, order processing, payment handling, and customer support via the e-commerce platform.
